About HobbySite

At Hobbysite, we are a team of developers who who also share a wide range of personal hobbies and creative interests, so we understand the value of having a dedicated space to express and grow those passions. This eventually inspired us to create a platform where individuals can showcase their arts and crafts, original works, analysis, discussions, and create a community that helps each other foster their hobbies. Our goal is to provide specific applications that can support our community and their needs, such as a merchandise store, wiki, blog, forum, and a commission board. After continuing on developing this platform, we decided to expand this website to include user inputs and functionalities to expand our user base.

Created by Kino De Torres, Wahab Iburahim, Ian Mercado, Alyssa Tan, and Mady Young.
